About Me

I am a Postdoc Associate at SMART Centre, MIT, working with Prof. Siqi Zheng. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in GIScience from Peking University in 2025, supervised by Prof. Zhou Huang and Prof. Yu Liu. I also received my B.S. degree in GIScience from Peking University in 2020.

My research interests focus on urban analytics with geospatial big data and advanced models, including sustainability issues like transportation and urbanization. My research goal is to introduce state-of-the-art theories and models to better understand the human-environment relationship.

Besides, I am also very interested in Hiking, Traveling, and Reading :）.
